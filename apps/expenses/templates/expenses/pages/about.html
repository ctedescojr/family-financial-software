About Family Finances

Family Finances is a smart and user-friendly web application designed to help families take control of their financial lives.

The platform enables you to track your fixed expenses and manage variable expenses from your shopping activities, all in one place.

One of the core features of Family Finances is the use of artificial intelligence to assist with shopping decisions. The system allows you to create shopping lists and suggests the most cost-effective places to buy, by calculating not only product prices but also travel distances and fuel costs using Google Maps integration.

You can even upload your receipts or invoices, and the system will automatically extract product information using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and AI models, helping you keep your expenses up to date effortlessly.

With Family Finances, you spend less time worrying about money and more time enjoying what matters most.
